Pizza

"A Pizza Maid"
To softly whisper, "...lovely Lisa..."
brings dreams of lips so full and red.
Which rhymes, of course, with "Garlic Bread".
And Lisa assonates with "Piece a Pizza."
If you do not speak as good
as proper speaking people should.
